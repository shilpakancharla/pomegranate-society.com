What is restorative justice?

Restorative justice aims to repair the harm caused by crimes. It strives for accountability and making amends between community members and for the potential to foster understanding within society. hen victims, survivors, perpetrators, and the communities meet together as a whole to discuss solutions, innovative and transformational solutions can possibly be created. Overall, it can serve as an opportunity for reconciliation.

Why value anonymity?

Choosing to remain anonymous can be important for many reasons. It offers protection and courage, and can allow those who have cultural conflicts and concerns to still express themselves without fear of judgement. It can serve as a crucial aspect in how someone wants to express their story.

What does the pomegranate represent?

Pomegranates are symbols across many cultures and often represent fertility, life, and power. These themes can be related to healing and transformation, and reclaiming your narrative by sharing your experiences.
