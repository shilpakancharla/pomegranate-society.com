Submission Form

Guidelines

  1. Please use content or trigger warnings at the beginning of your submissions as necessary.

  2. You may choose to remain anonymous by checking the box. When you do this, the name field will disappear.

  3. You may choose to be contacted regarding your submission. This is not necessary, however. If you check the box to be contacted, you will need to provide your email. This could be useful in case of typos found in your submission or letting you know when your submission is posted.

  4. Do not include actual names unless you have the explicit permission of the person whose name you are using. Our platform is not meant to be a call-out, but rather a call-for-action.

  5. Our goal is to foster an understanding and build empathy for those who do not necessarily have a platform. Please keep this goal in mind for any work that you submit.

  6. If the person is a public figure that has garnered previous media attention, then using their name without permission is acceptable.

  7. The maximum character limit is 10000 characters. This is roughly between 1000 and 2000 words.

  8. Poetry, recipes, and any kind of prose is accepted.

  9. If you have any links to other websites you want to include, you can just copy and paste them into the text field along with your submission.

  10. Currently, the software framework does not support image uploads. If you would like to include images for your post, email them to societypomegranate@gmail.com. This is a feature that is still being worked on. You can definitely still remain anonymous if you choose to email the organization the images. Sorry for this inconvenience, and thank you for your cooperation!

  11. Your submission will only be accepted if you check the 'Agree' box below. Checking this box confirms that you have read all the aforementioned rules.

  12. Any deviations from these rules may result in the delay of your submission being published.
